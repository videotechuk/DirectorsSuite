[center][size=6][b]DIRECTOR'S SUITE[/b][/size]
[size=4]Direct and edit video content in Red Dead Redemption 2[/size][/center]

[line]

[size=5][b]What is it?[/b][/size]

Red Dead Redemption 2 ships with no Rockstar Editor and no real video tools at all. [b]Director's Suite[/b] rebuilds the concept from the ground up on top of what the engine actually exposes — scripted cameras, the Photo Mode natives, the timecycle, weather and clock systems — into a single, creator-focused suite that feels closer to a lightweight film-production editor than a debug menu.

Stage a scene, light it, direct the cast, fly cinematic cameras, grade the look, and capture it — all inside a live game session.

[line]

[size=5][b]Photo Mode[/b][/size]
A full cinematic capture suite that replaces RDR2's limited native Photo Mode, with a clean tabbed interface. First-time hints explain each tab as you open it.

[list]
[*][b]Camera[/b] — free-fly and orbit any subject, full roll (dutch angles), FOV, lock-on tracking, and real Photo Mode depth of field (focus distance, blur, auto-focus on the subject).
[*][b]World[/b] — freeze the world, [b]Frame Forward[/b] single-stepping for exact timing, a time-of-day slider, instant weather and a moon override.
[*][b]Character[/b] — pick any nearby person or animal as the subject; 19 photo poses, facial expressions, [b]Look At Camera[/b], hide the subject or everyone else, and [b]become[/b] a story character. Plus a [b]Weapon Action[/b] (aim / fire) for action shots and a holster toggle that stops the body glitching when armed.
[*][b]Lighting[/b] — place scene lights by direct [b]X / Y / Z[/b] value, aim the [b]Sun[/b] with simple Direction & Height controls (with an on-screen sun-position compass so you always know where it is), and add the authentic Rockstar light rig or a character-following 3-point hero rig.
[*][b]Post[/b] — 16 real in-game color grades (cinematic, warm, cold, noir, film grain, vignette, lens distortion…), motion blur, cinematic aspect frames, a composition grid, plus the R* enhanced-render path with exposure/contrast trims.
[*][b]Effects[/b] — all 38 native Photo Mode filters, one-click reset, and lossless / HDR screenshots.
[*][b]Settings[/b] — free-cam speed and look sensitivity, shared with the main menu.
[/list]

[size=5][b]Scene Editor (Beta)[/b][/size]
A staging tool built into Photo Mode. Everything it creates is [b]temporary[/b] and is reverted the moment you leave Photo Mode — it never writes to disk or modifies game files.

[list]
[*][b]Object placement[/b] — browse or search a catalogue of ~16,950 props, spawn them in front of the lens, then position by direct [b]X / Y / Z[/b] value (plus rotation), ground-snap, duplicate, delete, freeze and toggle collision. A live selection box marks the object you're editing.
[*][b]Actors & scenarios[/b] — spawn from the full ~1,980-model ped catalogue (browse all humans / animals, or curated quick-picks) and assign [b]scenarios[/b] — the predefined action containers (smoking, guarding, sleeping, a dog eating, a horse drinking at a trough…). The huge scenario list is [b]categorised and validated[/b]: humans only ever see human scenarios, and an animal only sees scenarios for its own species — a dog never gets a horse's or a human's actions.
[*][b]Temporary world (YMAP) edits[/b] — clean up a composition without touching files: hide the map prop under the crosshair, hide every instance of a model, or force/spawn a normally-blocked map archetype, with an adjustable radius. Every edit is listed, individually undoable, and all restored on exit.
[/list]

[size=5][b]Camera System[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Insert, move, delete, enable/disable and edit up to [b]300[/b] cameras, with automatic naming.
[*]Camera modes (Follow, Look At, Same Angle, Free Angle), per-camera transitions with smoothing, zoom, shake, motion blur, DOF, per-camera weather/time and Photo Mode filter.
[*]Camera presets, full-project playback, automatic camera switching.
[*][b]One-click OBS recording[/b] over the obs-websocket 5.x protocol — Play Full Project starts the recording, plays the whole sequence, and stops at the end. Hands-free export.
[/list]

[size=5][b]Director Mode[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Spawn Story Mode characters, ambient townfolk, lawmen, gangs, workers and animals; compose with place-at-view, face camera/player, heading and nudging.
[*]NPC properties — health, invincibility, hostility, combat accuracy, weapon loadouts, outfits — plus tasks, scenarios and facial expressions.
[*]Hero lighting (authentic Rockstar light rigs + a custom 3-point rig) for the player and every NPC.
[*]World controls — pedestrian density and Clear Area sweeps to keep the shot clean.
[/list]

[size=5][b]Capture[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Lossless / HDR screenshots[/b] — saved straight from the display via Windows Desktop Duplication: a lossless PNG, or a 16-bit JPEG-XR when your display is in HDR. Saved to a [i]Captured Screenshots[/i] folder next to the game.
[*][b]Super-Resolution Capture (Test)[/b] — an experimental high-resolution mode that pans a zoomed camera across a grid, then reprojects and blends the tiles into one large, seamless image (2x or 3x your screen resolution). Best on a frozen scene.
[/list]

[size=5][b]Also includes[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Cinematic free-camera / placement mode with high-detail world streaming.
[*]Aiming modes and a configurable Aim Assist (body-part targeting, snap strengths, model filters).
[*]Time, weather and advanced cloud controls; gameplay options (instant kill, invincibility).
[*]A full in-game Help section.
[/list]

[size=5][b]Installation[/b][/size]
[list=1]
[*]Install [url=http://www.dev-c.com/rdr2/scripthookrdr2/]ScriptHookRDR2[/url] and an ASI loader.
[*]Copy [b]DirectorsSuite.asi[/b] and [b]DirectorsSuite.ini[/b] next to [b]RDR2.exe[/b]. (The prop, scenario and ped catalogues are compiled into the ASI — nothing else needs to ship.)
[*][i]Optional:[/i] copy the [b]Icons[/b] folder to [b]<game>\DirectorsSuite_Icons[/b] for the custom Photo Mode tab icons.
[*]Launch the game and open the menu.
[/list]
[b]Important:[/b] if you used an earlier build, delete the old [b]CreatorSuiteKit.asi[/b] (and its files) first — running two copies at once causes a doubled, overlapping menu.

[size=5][b]Default keys[/b][/size] [size=2](all rebindable in DirectorsSuite.ini)[/size]
[list]
[*][b]F2[/b] — open / close the menu
[*][b]F1[/b] — toggle Photo Mode
[*][b]INSERT[/b] — insert a camera at the current view (Placement Camera Mode starts from Camera Shots > Place a Camera)
[*][b]F3[/b] — lossless / HDR screenshot
[*][b]N / B[/b] — next / previous camera
[*][b]C[/b] (hold while aiming) — Aim Assist
[*][b]H[/b] — hide the UI for a clean shot (in Photo Mode)
[/list]

[list]
[*]Red Dead Redemption 2 (Story Mode)
[*]ScriptHookRDR2 + an ASI loader
[*]Windows 10 (1803+) for the lossless screenshot capture
[/list]
